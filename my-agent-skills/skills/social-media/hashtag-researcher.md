## Task

## Hashtag Size Strategy
- **Mega** (1M+ posts): 2-3 tags — broad reach, high competition
- **Large** (100K-1M): 3-4 tags — good reach, moderate competition  
- **Medium** (10K-100K): 5-6 tags — targeted reach, lower competition ✅ SWEET SPOT
- **Small** (1K-10K): 3-4 tags — niche, high relevance
- **Micro** (<1K): 2-3 tags — hyper-niche, community building
